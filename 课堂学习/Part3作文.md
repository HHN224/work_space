# 大学英语二级期末口语考试 · Part III 一分钟陈述

> 用法：考试时教师抽 1 个话题，允许看笔记。每篇约 150–165 词，朗读约 1 分 10 秒，1–2 分钟内可读完。用词尽量简单。每篇都贴合对应课文立场。

---

## Topic 1: My Career Plan
*(贴合 U3 课文 "Having a Finger in Many Pies" —— 田培珊多次创业：勇于尝试、早失败学到宝贵经验、创造让自己骄傲的东西而非只追高薪、长远愿景+坚持)*

My career plan is simple but clear. First, I want to choose a field that matches my skills and interests. Before I finish college, I will try internships in different fields, because I believe it is good to try many things early. Some tries may fail, but each small attempt teaches me lessons that books cannot.

Second, I do not just want a high salary. I want to build something I can feel proud of years later, just as the person who starts her own business does. To me, a meaningful career is about creating real value, not only earning money.

Finally, I know the road will not be easy. There will be stress and uncertainty. But I will keep a long-term vision and keep working hard, because I believe every effort counts. If I keep learning and never give up, I will find a career I truly enjoy.

## Topic 2: Three Indispensable Qualities of a Leader
*(贴合 U4 课文 "What Is Leadership?" —— Kevin Kruse：领导力 = 影响他人 + 激发他人最大努力 + 朝向共同目标；课文明说领导力≠头衔、≠资历、≠魅力、≠管理。所以这三个品质是"影响、激发、目标"，不能写"魅力/权威")*

In my opinion, three qualities make a great leader. The most important is the power to influence others. A real leader does not need a title, seniority, or charm. What matters is that others choose to follow because they trust the leader.

The second quality is the ability to bring out the best in others. A leader is not the same as a manager. A manager gives orders, but a leader helps the team give their full effort towards a shared goal.

The third quality is a clear goal. Without a goal, even strong influence has no direction. A leader must know where the team is going and guide everyone there together.

In short, leadership is a process of social influence that maximizes the efforts of others towards a goal. You do not need a big title or a loud voice to lead — you just need to influence, support, and guide your team.

## Topic 3: The Positive and Negative Impacts of AI on Our Lives
*(贴合 U5 课文 "Turning Your Phone Off as a Technological Gesture of Affection" —— 科技带来效率/便利为正面；制造"虚假陪伴感"、让人高估自己注意力、失去真实人际互动为负面；结论是明智使用、别让它取代真实关注。U6 课文是社会等级，与 AI 无关)*

AI has changed our lives in many ways. On the positive side, AI makes life easier and faster. It helps us search for information, translate languages, and get answers quickly. It saves time and helps us work more efficiently.

However, AI also brings problems. Just like our phones, AI can give us a feeling of company without real friendship. Real friendship is hard and needs real attention, but AI makes it seem easy. Also, when we rely on AI too much, we may think we can do many things at once, but in fact we lose focus. We may even stop talking to real people around us.

In conclusion, AI is a powerful tool, but we should use it wisely. We should never let it replace real human attention and real relationships.
